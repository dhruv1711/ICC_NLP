1. The Office of the Prosecutor (‘’Prosecution’’) herewith files on the case
record the disclosure note regarding the provision to the Defence team of
Laurent Gbagbo on 15 May 2012 of 62 documents pursuant to Article 67(2)
of the Statute.
2. Some of the documents disclosed on the above‐mentioned date contain
proprio motu redactions, including to their metadata, applied in accordance
with the Chamber’s decision of 24 January 20121. These redactions have been
applied to names of OTP staff, to exact locations and the day and month of
interviews, to specific contact information of witnesses, to the identity of
certain OTP sources or to information related to innocent third parties.
Request for Confidentiality
3. Annex A2 appended to this communication contains the disclosure note
provided to the Defence. The Prosecution requests that this annex be
received as ‘’Confidential’’ since it relates to inter partes disclosure.
