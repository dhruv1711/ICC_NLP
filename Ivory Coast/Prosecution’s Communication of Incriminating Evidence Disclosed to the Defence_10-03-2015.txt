1. The Office of the Prosecutor (“Prosecution” or “OTP”) notifies Trial Chamber I
(“Chamber”) of its disclosure, on 9 March 2015,1 of 114 incriminating items,2 10
potentially exculpatory items, 3 and 47 documents disclosed pursuant to rule 77 of
the Rules of Procedure and Evidence (“Rules”).4
2. Three documents 5 contained in this package were re-disclosed with less
redactions, pursuant to the decision of the Chamber dated 25 February 2015.6 The
remaining documents were disclosed pursuant to the Prosecution’s request of 6
February 2015 for an extension of time to disclose certain material.7 Document
CIV-OTP-0076-1509 disclosed as rule 77 of the Rules was provided pursuant to
the Prosecution’s ongoing disclosure obligation.
3. 16 items disclosed as incriminating and six items disclosed pursuant to rule 77 of
the Rules were disclosed with redactions to their image and/or metadata, in
accordance with decision ICC-02/11-01/11-737 and its Annex (“Redaction
Protocol”). 4. The disclosed material was provided in a format that complies with the eCourt
protocol as approved at the Pre-Trial stage.8
Request for Confidentiality
5. Annexes A,9 B10 and C11 appended to this communication contain the disclosure
notes provided to the Defence. The Prosecution requests that these annexes be
received as “Confidential’’ since they relate to inter partes disclosure.
