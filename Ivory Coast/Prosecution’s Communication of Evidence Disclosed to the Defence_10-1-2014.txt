1. The Office of the Prosecutor (‘’Prosecution’’) notifies Pre-Trial Chamber I
(“Chamber”) of its disclosure, on 9 January 2014, of 65 incriminating documents 1
and one Rule 77 document.
2 These documents were disclosed pursuant to the
Chamber’s decision of 8 January 2014.
3
2. Out of the 65 documents disclosed as incriminating documents, 18 were disclosed
with redactions to the content, pursuant to Rule 81(4) and/or to the metadata
pursuant to Rule 81(2).
3. The one document disclosed pursuant to Rule 77 is disclosed with proprio motu
redactions to the content and the metadata. The redactions were applied to the
name of OTP staff and to the day and month of interview. The proprio motu
redactions were applied pursuant to paragraph 51 of Decision ICC-02/11-01/11-30.
4. The disclosed material was provided in a format that complies with the eCourt
protocol approved by the Chamber.
